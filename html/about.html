About me

What is this Website

This website is designed to provide a single account system where users can register their game and CTF scores, view leaderboards, and play with friends when multiplayer games are available. It serves as a hub for tracking progress and fostering friendly competition. Overall, this project is a personal endeavor aimed at learning new technologies while having fun along the way.

CTF section

Capture the Flag (CTF) competitions are a fun and engaging way to learn cybersecurity skills. Participants solve challenges that often involve cryptography, web security, reverse engineering, and more. CTFs are designed to be both educational and competitive, providing a platform to test your skills against others while learning new techniques. They are enjoyable because they combine problem-solving with teamwork and creativity, making them a productive way to enhance your technical abilities.

Games section
